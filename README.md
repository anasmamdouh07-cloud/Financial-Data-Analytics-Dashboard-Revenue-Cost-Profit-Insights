# Financial Data Analytics Dashboard | Revenue, Cost & Profit Insights

## Overview

This project is an end-to-end Financial Data Analytics solution designed to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights.

The project covers the complete analytics workflow, from data extraction and validation to transformation, visualization, and presentation.

## Objectives

* Analyze Revenue, Cost, Profit, and Margin performance.
* Identify profitability trends across countries and product categories.
* Discover key drivers impacting business performance.
* Deliver insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

## Key Business Insights

### Revenue Growth

Revenue increased from 0.49M in 2010 to 33.57M in 2013, demonstrating significant business growth.

### Profitability Challenge

Despite record revenue in 2013, profitability declined due to increasing costs.

### Top Performing Markets

The United States and Canada generated the highest profits.

### Underperforming Markets

Germany, Australia, and France reported negative profitability.

### Cost Driver Analysis

The Bikes category accounted for approximately 84% of total costs, making it the primary area for margin improvement.

### Volume vs Revenue

Sales volume grew faster than revenue, indicating increasing pricing pressure.
